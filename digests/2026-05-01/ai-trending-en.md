**Skills Framework Commoditization**: The simultaneous emergence of [mattpocock/skills](https://github.com/mattpocock/skills), [superpowers](https://github.com/obra/superpowers), and [browserbase/skills](https://github.com/browserbase/skills) reveals a **standardization race for agent capabilities**. These aren't agent frameworks but *capability packaging systems*—analogous to npm for JavaScript or crates for Rust. The [everything-claude-code](https://github.com/affaan-m/everything-claude-code) project's 170K stars validates this as a mature ecosystem layer, with skills now treated as **performance-critical infrastructure** requiring optimization (instincts, memory, security).

**Vertical Agent Specialization**: [TradingAgents](https://github.com/TauricResearch/TradingAgents) and [waoowaoo](https://github.com/saturndec/waoowaoo) demonstrate agents moving beyond general coding into **domain-specific long-horizon workflows**—financial markets and film production respectively. Both use multi-agent architectures with specialized roles, suggesting "agent teams" as the next architectural pattern.

**Vectorless RAG Emergence**: [PageIndex](https://github.com/VectifyAI/PageIndex)'s "vectorless, reasoning-based RAG" and [cognee](https://github.com/topoteretes/cognee)'s knowledge engine signal potential **fatigue with embedding-based retrieval**. **Connection to Industry Events**: The timing aligns with anticipated Q2 2026 releases—Kimi-K2.5 and GLM-5 support in [Ollama](https://github.com/ollama/ollama), gpt-oss integration across multiple projects, and the "Claude Code" ecosystem maturation suggest **Anthropic's agent-centric strategy is winning developer mindshare**.
